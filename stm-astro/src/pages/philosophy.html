Philosophy of Matters

Mission

To maximize our positive impact through scalable solutions, and investment that aligns with our philosophy.

Our Philosophy

At Matters, we align our work with our values — recognizing that “doing good” is complex. While we don't assert a universal definition, we draw from patterns across moral philosophies, spiritual traditions, and social and cognitive science. Our shared principles are:

  • A drive toward altruism and generosity

  • Fostering connection, relationships, and social harmony

  • Stewarding kindness and justice

They shape what we build, who we build with, and how we measure success, while uniting our efforts to benefit people, other living beings, and the planet.

What we work on

We focus on overlooked or underserved problems — especially where existing systems fall short and our contribution can uniquely create value and drive impact.

How we work

We operate as a learning engine — deeply researched in our convictions but humble in our validations. We use rapid testing, structured frameworks, and rigorous feedback loops to build faster, learn better, and serve more effectively.

Why we work this way

We feel a responsibility to take on challenges others may not have the opportunity to, and that we're serving our purpose by doing so
